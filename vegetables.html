Vegetable Grain

Description

  • Nutritious vegetables and wholesome grains.
  • This dish features vibrant bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes, all sautéed with garlic and herbs to enhance their natural flavors.
  • Mixed with a base of fluffy quinoa or nutty brown rice, the grains absorb the savory goodness of the vegetables.
  • The result? A satisfying, nutrient-packed meal that’s as delightful to look at as it is to eat.
  • Perfect for a balanced lunch or dinner!

Vegetable Grain Ingredients:

  • Quinoa or Brown Rice
  • Bell Peppers (red, yellow, green)
  • Zucchini
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Spinach
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Herbs (Basil, Oregano, Thyme)
  • Lemon Juice
